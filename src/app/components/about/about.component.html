Game rules

The game area is divided into 3 zones: skyline, along which ships move; sea area, divided into cells, at which the shot is fired and the area, along which the sight moves.

Your task is to shoot down as many ships as possible. The sight is controlled by the left and right keys on the keyboard, the shot is fired by the Enter key. Before starting the game, you will be prompted to select the size of the game area and the game mode.
